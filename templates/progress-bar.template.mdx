## Overview

The Progress Bar component visualizes the completion status of tasks, processes, or operations. It's ideal for showing upload/download progress, form completion, loading states, onboarding steps, or any process that unfolds over time.

The component offers flexible label positioning options (right, bottom, top-floating, bottom-floating) to accommodate different layouts and design needs. It supports multiple color variants (brand, error, success, warning, default) to convey different statesâ€”such as normal progress, completion success, or error conditions. The component includes a customizable value formatter that allows you to display progress as percentages, fractions, or custom text formats. With configurable min/max ranges and additional styling options through className props, the Progress Bar adapts to various use cases while providing clear visual feedback about task completion status.
